Welcome to the new and improved Brinnington Big Local website where you can find out what are doing to support our community, located in the heart of brinnington we have our hub where we host groups, arrange events and provide support for residents.The Big Local is a lottery-funded scheme to make lasting change in disadvantaged areas, creating a better place to live.

Brinnington Big Local is a community-led group consisting of passionate and skilled local people, working together to meet the needs of the Brinnington area. The group’s priorities are determined by community consultation and engagement, targeting issues such as food poverty, anti-social behaviour, financial hardship, domestic abuse, unemployment and health and wellbeing.

Find out more by visiting our about and Projects pages


Banter and Brews Thursday Meeting

Today, we're excited to invite you to the Banter and Brews Thursday meeting. Join us at the Community Hub from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM for a warm and welcoming gathering.

At Banter and Brews, we provide a safe and supportive space where men of all ages can come together. We offer good old-fashioned banter, hot food, and a variety of beverages to ensure your time with us is enjoyable. Whether you're feeling isolated, lonely, anxious, or just bored, you'll be made welcome. Join us today and be a part of our vibrant community.
