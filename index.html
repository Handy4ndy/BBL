Brinnington Big Local

Welcome to the new and improved Brinnington Big Local website where you can find out what are doing to support our community, located in the heart of brinnington we have our hub where we host groups, arrange events and provide support for residents.

The Big Local is a lottery-funded scheme to make lasting change in disadvantaged areas, creating a better place to live. Brinnington Big Local is a community-led group consisting of passionate and skilled local people, working together to meet the needs of the Brinnington area. Brinnington Big Local is more than just an initiative - it's a movement towards positive change. Through collaboration, education, and community-driven initiatives, we're transforming Brinnington into a place where everyone's voice is heard and everyone plays a role in its growth.

Whether you're a long-time resident or new to the area, Brinnington Big Local has something for you. Join our vibrant community, engage in meaningful projects, and shape the future of Brinnington. Together, we can create a neighborhood that we're all proud to call home.
