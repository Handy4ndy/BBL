Welcome to new and improved Brinnington Big Local website.

The Big Local is a lottery-funded scheme to make lasting change in disadvantaged areas, creating a better place to live. Brinnington Big Local is a community-led group consisting of passionate and skilled local people, working together to meet the needs of the Brinnington Area.

We are passionate about engaging and involving local people, Finding out what is important to them as individuals and groups to utilize the information to help shape projects that will benifit everyone in our community.

Brinnington Big Local is more than just an initiative - it's a movement towards positive change. Through collaboration, education, and community-driven initiatives, we're transforming Brinnington into a place where everyone's voice is heard and everyone plays a role in its growth.

Whether you're a long-time resident or new to the area, Brinnington Big Local has something for you. Join our vibrant community, engage in meaningful projects, and shape the future of Brinnington. Together, we can create a neighborhood that we're all proud to call home.

Ready to be a part of something bigger? Welcome to Brinnington Big Local - where community collaboration brings positive change and transforms dreams into reality.
