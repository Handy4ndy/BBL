Welcome to the new and improved Brinnington Big Local website where you can find out what are doing to support our community, located in the heart of brinnington we have our hub where we host groups, arrange events and provide support for residents.

The Big Local is a lottery-funded scheme to make lasting change in disadvantaged areas, creating a better place to live.

Brinnington Big Local is a community-led group consisting of passionate and skilled local people, working together to meet the needs of the Brinnington area.
