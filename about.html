The Big Local is a lottery-funded scheme aimed at creating lasting change in disadvantaged areas, making them better places to live. Brinnington Big Local is a community-led group comprising passionate and skilled local individuals dedicated to meeting the needs of the Brinnington area.

Our mission is to engage and involve local people, listening to their individual and group concerns to shape projects that benefit our community. We're committed to creating a safer, greener environment, enhancing personal health and well-being, and supporting local learning and upskilling.

Key Features of Big Local:

  • Long-term - Providing certainty and continuity over 10 years.
  • Resident-led - Working directly with individuals in the community to build confidence and capacity.
  • Non-prescriptive - Empowering residents to spend on projects they deem important.
  • Patient and non-judgmental - Giving communities time to learn, make mistakes, and overcome challenges.
