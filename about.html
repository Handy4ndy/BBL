Our Mission

At Brinnington Big Local, we are committed to creating a thriving and connected community. Our mission is to make a lasting impact in Brinnington, enhancing the quality of life for residents.

Current Initiatives

Explore our ongoing initiatives aimed at addressing various community needs. We are actively working to combat issues such as food poverty, anti-social behavior, financial hardship, domestic abuse, unemployment, and health and wellbeing.

Past Achievements

Reflect on the milestones we've achieved in the past. From hosting successful events to establishing groups like Green Thumbs and the Orange Umbrella project, we have a track record of turning aspirations into reality.

Explore Volunteering Opportunities

Our Volunteers are the unsung heroes, the heart and soul of our community initiatives. They are individuals who generously invest their precious time, unwavering dedication, and unique expertise in making a positive impact on the lives of others. These remarkable individuals are the driving force behind many of our essential tasks.

From registering new members to preparing nutritious and delicious packed lunches for our young community members, our Volunteers take on a myriad of roles. Their dedication knows no bounds as they work tirelessly behind the scenes to ensure our programs and events run seamlessly. We operate as a closely-knit team where every contribution, no matter how small, plays a vital role in our collective efforts to make a profound difference in our community.

Our Partners: Together We Make Change Happen

At Brinnington Big Local, we believe in the power of collaboration. We're proud to work hand in hand with a network of like-minded organizations and passionate individuals who share our vision for a better community.

Our partners are the heartbeat of our collective efforts. They bring diverse expertise, fresh perspectives, and endless dedication to the table, making every project more impactful and meaningful.

By joining forces with local groups, institutions, and community leaders, we create a united front, resilient and empowered to tackle the challenges of our area. Together, we turn dreams into tangible projects and make a lasting difference where it matters most.
