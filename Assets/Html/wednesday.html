Citizens Advice: Every Wednesday

Every Wednesday, Citizens Advice is here to assist you with a range of issues, from housing problems and debt concerns to unemployment and benefit claims. Visit us at "The Hub" from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM. No appointments necessary – just drop in!

We're here to help Brinnington residents gain financial stability, and we've already assisted over 170 individuals with more than 270 issues. With your support, we've helped residents gain £29,589. Let's work together to improve your quality of life and address the challenges you face.

JobFit: Employment Support

Join us at "The Hub" every Wednesday for our JobFit employment support sessions. We're here to assist you on your journey to find meaningful employment. Whether you're a regular visitor or it's your first time, we're excited to have you.

At JobFit, we're committed to helping you enhance your job-seeking skills, find opportunities, and connect with the resources you need. Join us for an evening of support, camaraderie, and insights to boost your employment prospects. Don't miss out on this chance to improve your job search!
